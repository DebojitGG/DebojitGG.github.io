Please following steps to install mooFacebook solution:

1. Download installation package at client area here https://clients.moosocial.com
2. Unzip and then upload all files and folders in to your hosting server
3. Follow the installation steps here https://moosocial.com/knowledge-base/install-moosocial/ to install
5. Configure your mooFacebook site follow some useful resources here https://moosocial.com/knowledge-base
